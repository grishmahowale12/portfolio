    CyberThreat Response Predictor

      Our team built a predictive model to forecast how long it takes to contain and resolve different types of cyberattacks ranging from ransomware to insider threats. I led the data preprocessing phase, which involved cleaning and preparing a dataset of 3,000+ real-world incidents. This step was crucial to ensure accurate insights by handling missing values, formatting features, and establishing a solid foundation for the machine learning pipeline. While the tool is still evolving, it holds great promise in supporting IT teams to better allocate resources and minimize operational disruption during breaches.
